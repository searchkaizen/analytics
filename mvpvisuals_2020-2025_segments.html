Disclaimer
This report uses Shopify referring-channel exports to summarize orders and revenue where Shopify attributed the sale to a search engine referral source. These figures reflect Shopify’s last non-direct click referral attribution, not a full view of the customer journey. A customer may have interacted with other channels before purchase, so these figures should not be interpreted as definitive causal impact from SEO. Session-based comparisons are not available for 2020-2021 because Shopify’s export does not include usable session data for those years.
