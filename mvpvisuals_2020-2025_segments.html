Strategic Takeaways
Key Findings
📈 Momentum

Organic revenue grew +51.5% YoY in 2025 — the strongest single-year improvement in the dataset. Sessions grew faster (+59%), signaling continued indexing gains yet to fully convert.

⚠️ CVR Gap

Conversion rate remains at 0.195% — flat vs. 2024 and far below 2022's 1.78%. All organic growth is volume-driven. CVR optimization is the highest-leverage next action.

🤖 AI Referral Traffic

ChatGPT appeared as a revenue-generating referrer for the first time in 2025: ~$8,500 across 8 orders (3 channels combined). AOV of $1,100+ — high-quality buyers from AI assistants.

🔍 Bing Organic

Bing delivers $22,182 in revenue at a $1,052 AOV — higher than Google organic. Low volume (7K sessions) but highly qualified buyer intent. Consistently growing year over year.

📉 2023 Trough

Google organic revenue dropped -80% from 2021 to 2023 despite sessions growing 5x. This "traffic without intent" gap has since corrected but the CVR floor remains structurally low.

🏷️ Long-Tail Performers

Dealspotr ($6.8K, 15 orders), Knoji ($3.4K), and Menlosecurity ($10.1K at $3,239 AOV) punch well above their session counts — niche referrers driving premium buyers.
